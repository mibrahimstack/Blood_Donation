Your blood donation can save up to three lives. Join our community of everyday heroes today.

About Us: Why Donate?

Every two seconds, someone needs blood. Your contribution is absolutely vital for surgeries, cancer treatments, and emergencies.

Eligibility: Who Can Donate?

Most healthy adults over the age of 18 weighing at least 50kg can donate. Check our detailed eligibility guidelines to learn more.

The Process

Donating is safe, simple, and takes about 45 minutes from registration to refreshments. Your body replenishes the fluids quickly.
